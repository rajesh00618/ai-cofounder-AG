# AI Co-Founder — Features

## 1. AI Reality Engine
Evaluates startup goals for feasibility across 8 dimensions (Market, Team, Product, Traction, Competition, Financials, Timing, Risk). Generates a Reality Score (0–100%), radar chart, risk list, and AI recommendation. Low-scoring goals enter Negotiation where the AI suggests alternatives with better success probability.

## 2. Business Blueprint Generator
Automatically generates a complete 15-section business blueprint after goal setting: Executive Summary, Problem, Solution, Target Customer, Competitors, Revenue Model, Go-to-Market, MVP Plan, Validation Plan, Risks, Success Metrics, Timeline, Funding Needs, Team, Milestones. Editable and exportable.

## 3. Full Execution Plan
AI generates a multi-phase execution plan from start to goal, with each phase having a title, goal, duration, and 3–5 tasks with priority, estimated time, and difficulty. Automatically creates one sprint per phase. First sprint's tasks appear as Today's Tasks. Respects the goal timeframe (e.g., a 30-day goal generates a plan fitting inside 30 days).

## 4. AI Co-Founder Chat
10 specialized AI agents with distinct personalities and expertise:
- **CEO** — Strategic vision, high-level decisions
- **CTO** — Architecture, technical decisions
- **CMO** — Marketing strategy, positioning
- **Sales** — Customer acquisition, sales process
- **Finance** — Financial planning, runway
- **Legal** — Compliance, IP, contracts
- **Research** — Market analysis, competitive intelligence
- **Designer** — UX, brand, product design
- **Developer** — Implementation, technical execution
- **Planner** — Task planning, project management

## 5. Founder DNA
AI analyzes your founder profile and generates scores across 8 dimensions (Risk Tolerance, Innovation, Leadership, Execution, Resilience, Adaptability, Strategic Thinking, Emotional Intelligence). Radar chart visualization. AI suggests adaptations based on your patterns.

## 6. Memory Graph
Visual knowledge graph connecting ideas, tasks, customers, documents, milestones, revenue, goals, and projects. Relationships include depends_on, influences, blocks, enables, contradicts. Navigate and inspect nodes for deeper business insights.

## 7. Research Center
AI-driven market research with capabilities:
- Company research (market position, competitors, financials)
- Market analysis (trends, TAM, growth projections)
- Morning briefing (daily AI-generated business briefing)
- Opportunity identification

## 8. Document Generator
Generate professional documents from your blueprint:
- Business Plan
- PRD (Product Requirements Document)
- Pitch Deck
- Executive Summary
- Marketing Plan
- Technical Spec
- SWOT Analysis
- Competitor Analysis

## 9. AI Board Meeting
Multi-agent board meeting where CEO, CTO, CMO, Sales, Finance, and Legal agents debate your current challenges. Each agent speaks in character with domain-specific reasoning. Chat history is maintained across sessions.

## 10. Investor Mode
AI evaluates your startup from an investor's perspective. Generates a detailed analysis with strengths, weaknesses, recommendation, and estimated valuation range. Useful for pitch preparation.

## 11. Decision Simulator
Test business decisions before making them:
- **Consequence Prediction** — AI predicts outcomes of a decision
- **Customer Reaction** — Simulates feedback from virtual customer personas
- **Failure Analysis** — Calculates failure probability and lists risks

## 12. Execution Mode
AI autonomously executes tasks from your plan. Steps through each task with reasoning, output, and status updates. Useful for routine or well-defined tasks.

## 13. Command Center Dashboard
Central dashboard showing:
- **Live Startup Score** — 6 metrics (Execution, Business, Customers, Product, Cash, AI Confidence)
- **Today's Mission** — AI-generated daily focus
- **Business Health** — Overall % + category breakdown
- **Today's Tasks** — Pending tasks from the active sprint (max 4)
- **AI Recommendation** — Contextual advice
- **Current Stage** — Stage tracker (Idea → Validation → MVP → Launch → Revenue → PMF → Scale)

## 14. Daily & Weekly Reviews
- **Daily Review** — Check in on what you accomplished and plan tomorrow
- **Weekly Review** — AI-generated performance summary with trends and stage guidance

## 15. Roadmap
Timeline view of your startup journey with stages, milestones, and progress indicators.

## 16. Task Engine
Full sprint and task management with:
- Sprint creation and tracking
- Task assignment (priority, difficulty, estimated time)
- Phase-sprint matching for full execution plan
- Today's Tasks from active sprint

## 17. Telegram Reminders
- Register Telegram chat ID in Settings
- Morning reminder (9 AM) with today's pending tasks
- Evening reminder (6 PM) asking if you completed your tasks
- Test button to verify delivery immediately

## 18. Settings
- API key configuration
- Telegram reminders registration
- Account management
- Server status monitoring

## 19. Security & Reliability
- JWT authentication with 7-day tokens
- Rate limiting on auth and API routes
- Helmet security headers
- CORS origin validation
- Prompt injection protection
- Circuit breaker for AI model fallback
- Input sanitization
- Error message sanitization (no stack leaks)
- Graceful shutdown handling
